This is my explanation on how my challenge went when working with stable diffusion models for the first time.



• Step 1: Understanding Diffusion Models


We started with the research of understanding diffusion models.

This was really interesting. This explanation was the most clear and helped me with understanding the main use of stable diffusion.



• Step 2: Methods for Fine-Tuning Stable Diffusion


There were a lot of different Fine-Tining models that we could have chosen. But we found one that worked for us. This was very problematic at first because, the whole team thought we had to do it in Visual Studio Code. In the end I found out that we had a JupyterLab environment running for us. This was a game changer and we were now finally able to start. Without any GPU or CPU errors that we first encountered in Visual Studio Code.


The model that worked for us was given by the Data Camp tutorial. This tutorial was easy to follow and gave us a lot of posibilities to improve our model.
